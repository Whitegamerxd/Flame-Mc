Server Features

SMP Developed by NOTFIRE

Our Survival Multiplayer (SMP) server is designed and created by NOTFIRE, bringing you the ultimate Minecraft experience with unique gameplay features and a community-driven environment.

Lifesteal Gameplay

Experience the thrill of **Lifesteal** gameplay, where every player’s health is intertwined with their combat prowess. Steal life from your enemies and survive against the toughest challenges.

Custom Plugins

We’ve implemented custom plugins to enhance your gameplay, creating a dynamic and exciting Minecraft experience with features that you won’t find anywhere else.

Friendly Community

Join a community that values collaboration, creativity, and fun! Our players are always ready to help each other and engage in exciting server events.

Regular Events & Challenges

Stay engaged with our regular in-game events and challenges. From PvP tournaments to building competitions, there’s always something exciting happening at FLAME MC.

Advanced Economy System

Our advanced economy system allows you to trade, create shops, and build your wealth. Buy, sell, and become a rich entrepreneur on the server!
